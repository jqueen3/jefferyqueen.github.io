Target Audience

The target audience for this site is fathers in South Western Idaho, or anyone else in that area needing to know the weather

Personas

  1. Dad age 42.
  2. Grandpa age 76.

Scenarios

  1. Dad wanting to reduce the cost of the electric bill searching to see if the weather is nice enough to open the windows.
  2. Dad planning a family outing wanting to know if a hike is reasonable according to the weather.
  3. Grandpa planning to take his eldest grandchild fishing needing to tell his daughter what to dress the grandchild in.
  4. Dad wanting his family to stop touching the thermostat deciding the best temperature to set it at based on the outside temperature.
  5. A storm is approaching, and the user wants to know if their house is in the path.
